This 3D minesweeper playground features a unique rendition of the classical game of minesweeper in 3D. Mines are spread on a 8x8x8 cube surface. The cube can be explored via pan, zoom, rotation and touch. A short tap on a tile will reveal the number adjacent mines, if it is empty. If it has a mine you will lose. A long tap on a tile will mark it with a flag. Tapping it again will remove the flag. Pressing the yellow bulb hint button on the top right will provide you a hint, if one is available. A red sphere will hint a mine tile while a green sphere will hint a safe tile. The menu bar has a green restart button, number of mine slider, a yellow hint button, a timer and remaining mine counter. Turn on the volume. Stay safe and have fun.
